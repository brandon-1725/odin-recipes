Fish and Chips Recipe

This easy beer-battered recipe for fried fish is perfectly crispy on the outside and tender and flakey on the inside. Pair it with french fries and homemade tartar sauce and fresh fries for a truly remarkable meal! Found on Tastes Better from Scratch.

Ingredients

  • Fish, preferably white fish
  • Beer
  • Baking Powder
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat Oil
  2. Prepare Fish
  3. Make Batter
  4. Dip Fish in Batter
  5. Fry Fish
  6. Remove to Paper Towel
