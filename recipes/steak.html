Steak Recipe

This sirloin steak recipe is served with very garlicky butter that makes this steak melt-in-your-mouth wonderful! Found on All Recipes.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • Garlic powder
  • Beef top sirloin
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat grill
  2. Melt butter and stir in garlic in a saucepan
  3. Season steak with salt and pepper
  4. Place steaks on grill for 4-5 minutes per side
  5. Brush steaks with butter
  6. Rest for 2-3 minutes
